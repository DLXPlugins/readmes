Easily create Todoist tasks from Gravity Forms submissions.

== Description ==

HeyTodos connects your Gravity Forms to Todoist. With just a few clicks, you can automatically turn Gravity Forms submissions into Todoist tasks.

**Features:**

* Connect Gravity Forms to Todoist.
* Map form fields to task content and description.
* Choose Todoist project, section, priority, due date, and labels.
* Works with both personal and team workspaces.
* Dynamic projects and sections with merge tags.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to `/wp-content/plugins/gf-hey-todos`, or install via the Plugins screen.
2. Activate the plugin through the "Plugins" screen in WordPress.
3. Go to *Forms → Settings → Todoist* to connect your Todoist account.
4. Create a Todoist feed in your form settings and map the fields you want.
